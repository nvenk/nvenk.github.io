Helping Sales people stay informed about their goals.

Over the summer of 2016, I interned at Xactly Corporation as a UX Designer. Being one of the two designers in the company, I was involved in a variety of products and exclusively handled design for two products that directly contributed to Xactly's user experience.

Xactly is an enterprise SaaS company, and is a market leader in providing incentive compensation solutions for employee and sales performance management.

While I was at Xactly, I worked with product management and engineering on a number of projects, acting as the design lead for two of them.

Xactly Incent is a platform for companies to manage sales performance and incentive compensation. The Android application is geared towards the end users, enabling them to get an overview of their performance and quotas.
