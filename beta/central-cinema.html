Central Cinema

Redesigning the ticket booking experience for a theatre.

Read About It

We've all seen some incredible movies that were released years ago and wished we could have watched them on the big screen. This is exactly what Central Cinema is all about. Located in Seattle's Central District, Central Cinema was a neighborhood "beer-theatre". As you entered the venue, you are hit with a sense of nostaliga - from the decor and the posters of cult classics on the walls, to the movies that were playing.

We set out to revamp their website and create an experience that was more in line with the experience they offered.

Our first meeting with the client was to understand his motivations and business goals, specific goals with the website redesign, and to define our project scope.

Ultimately, his primary goal with the new website was to get more people to book tickets online and in advance.
