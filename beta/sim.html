SIM: The Social Robot

Designing for affective Human-Robot Interaction.

Read About It

Robots have penetrated into our society at an astonishing rate over the past fifteen years. What started as machines performing repetitive tasks in industrial settings evolved into single-purpose machines for households tasks, and has more recently turned into dozens of different variations of robots that aim to closely integrate into our daily lives.

We thought it was important to re-examine our interactions with these technologies and understand how we can design more natural and intuitive interactions.
